How to Play

Quick guide to controls, tips, and dev tools.

Controls

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Punch: A, Kick: S, Jump: D
  • Pause/Resume: ESC / P, Mute: M
  • Run: Double-tap Left/Right then hold the direction

Boss Tips

  • Line up lanes: keep your Y close to the boss (within a small vertical band).
  • Attack window stays active briefly—step into range during the swing to land hits.
  • Use kicks for more damage; punches are quicker.
  • Watch the HP bar (enabled automatically when the boss spawns).

Dev Tools

  • Dev button (bottom-right) toggles options (Visual Debug, Hitbox Tuner).
  • Hitbox Tuner: enable to draw the red player attack box even without full Visual Debug.
  • Visual Debug: draw player/enemy/boss hurtboxes and last attack rectangles.
  • Spawn Boss: quickly start a fight near the player.
